Rakuten 返现日报｜2026-05-02

一句话结论：5/2 周六处于 Big Deal Reveal 月末档（4/29 切档延续到 5/3）与 Big Give Week（5/4–5/11）之间的"过渡周末"，主页 Midweek Madness 翻倍档稳定输出 PetSmart 15% / Ancestry 12% / EyeBuyDirect ≤10% / JD Sports 8% / Hollister 7% / Lowe’s ≤6% / Carter’s 6% / Canon 5%；两天后 Big Give Week 启动，提前把购物车备好可吃 15%/15x 一周大档，叠加 $40 新会员礼。

摘要

  • 过渡周末：Big Deal Reveal 4/29 这档延续至 5/3 23:59 ET，新一档与 Big Give Week 5/4 同步启动。今日主页主推 Midweek Madness 翻倍。
  • 翻倍档实测（rakuten.com 主页 5/2 抓取）：PetSmart 15% (was 2%)、Ancestry 12% (was 6%)、EyeBuyDirect ≤10% (was 5%)、Tripadvisor 8% (was 4%)、Target Optical 8% (was 4%)、JD Sports 8% (was 3%)、Hollister 7% (was 2%)、Lowe’s ≤6% (was 2%)、Carter’s 6% (was 2%)、ASICS 6% (was 3%)、CVSPhoto 6% (was 3%)、Canon 5% (was 2.5%)、Kay Jewelers 4% (was 2%)、StubHub 4% (was 2%)、adidas ≤4% (was 2%)。
  • 店内 in-store：49ers Team Store 10%、Zadig&Voltaire 8% (was 4%)、ASICS 店内 6% (was 3%)、Raymour & Flanigan 4% (was 2%)。
  • 倒计时事件：Big Give Week 5/4–5/11 全站 hundreds of stores 上 15%/15x，含 Adidas / Ulta / DSW / LEGO / Expedia / PetSmart / Ray-Ban / KitchenAid / Viator / Sonos / LG / Lenovo / Bombas / Bluemercury / Dermstore / Tatcha / Frye / Kate Spade Outlet / Sandro / Puma / Office Depot / SoFi / Verizon 等。
  • 新会员礼：Big Give Week 期内新会员 $40 / 4K MR / 4K Bilt（消费 $40 触发）。
